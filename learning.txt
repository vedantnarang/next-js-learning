INTERCEPTING ROUTES

    INTERCEPTING routes will let you show a page inside a current layout instead on navigating away to another page 
    for example photo gallery app 
    when you click on an image then instead of redirecting to /gallery/{id} we will show the image in the current layout kind of like a preview page

    HOW TO ??
    (.) same level
    (..) one level up 
    (..)(..) two levels up
    (...) from root level
